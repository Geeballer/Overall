About Us

Who we are, Our Mission & Values,

With a clear focus on Innovative solutions by and for the people. It is the deeper meaning behind our work, it is what drives us.

Who we are

August Mien Property Inspection company is the premier tech-Driven Inspection service company in Africa and the First Gig-economy driven property inspection company in the world.

We are here to disrupt the embedded home transaction process by creating a trust economy which will promote transparency and accountability in the real estate industry in Africa.

Our Mission

To be the TRUST platform that generates an Independent, Fair and Standard Assessment of properties to clients/subscribers.

To be the professional “eagle eye” and voice of reason of the client in the decision-making process in a real estate transaction

Our Vision

To be the world’s most trusted opinion in property transactions.

Our Values

Trust

We are Fair in our assessment of a property

Value

We deliver a greater amount of information that will help you make the best decision in negotiation on your property

Independent

We are not influenced by any external elements in the delivery of our professional duties
